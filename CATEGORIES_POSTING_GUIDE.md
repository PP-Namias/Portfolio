## 📝 Category Creation Process

### For Each Category:

#### **Title Field**

- Use exact titles listed above
- Keep it clear and professional
- 2-4 words maximum
- Title case formatting

#### **Slug Field**

- Will auto-generate from title
- Verify it matches recommended slugs
- Keep lowercase with hyphens
- No special characters

#### **Description Field**

- Copy the provided descriptions
- 2-3 sentences maximum
- Include relevant keywords
- Focus on value to readers

---

## 🎨 Content Guidelines

### Title Best Practices

- ✅ **Use**: Clear, descriptive names
- ✅ **Keep**: Consistent with your expertise
- ❌ **Avoid**: Vague terms like "Other" or "Misc"
- ❌ **Avoid**: Overlapping category purposes

### Description Writing Tips

1. **Start with Action**: "Explore", "Learn", "Discover"
2. **Include Keywords**: Relevant to your niche
3. **Show Value**: What readers will gain
4. **Stay Focused**: Specific to category purpose
5. **Maintain Tone**: Professional but approachable

## 🚨 Common Mistakes to Avoid

### Content Mistakes

- ❌ **Too Many Categories**: Keep to 6-8 maximum
- ❌ **Overlapping Purpose**: Clear distinction between categories
- ❌ **Generic Names**: "Blog", "Posts", "Articles"
- ❌ **No Descriptions**: Missing SEO opportunity

### Technical Mistakes

- ❌ **Special Characters**: In titles or slugs
- ❌ **Inconsistent Naming**: Mixed formatting styles
- ❌ **Duplicate Slugs**: Each must be unique
- ❌ **Long Descriptions**: Keep under 200 words
