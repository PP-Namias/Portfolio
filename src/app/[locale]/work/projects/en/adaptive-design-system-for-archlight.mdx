## Overview

The Pre-enrollment Management System is designed to streamline the pre-enrollment process for students. It allows for efficient data processing, storage, and management, tailored specifically for the Young Achievers' School of Caloocan, Inc. (YASC).

## Key Features

- **Data Management**: Efficiently process and store student pre-enrollment data.
- **User-Friendly Interface**: Designed for ease of use by school administrators.
- **Customizable**: Tailored to meet the specific needs of YASC.

## Technologies Used

- **VB.NET**: For building the application.
- **SQL**: For managing and storing data.

## Outcome

The system has successfully modernized the pre-enrollment process, reducing administrative overhead and improving data accuracy.
