## Photography Ethics

### Respectful Wildlife Photography
- **Safe Distance:** Using telephoto lenses to avoid disturbing animals
- **Natural Behavior:** Capturing authentic moments without interference
- **Habitat Protection:** Supporting conservation through responsible tourism
- **Educational Value:** Sharing knowledge about wildlife conservation

## Technical Approach

### Equipment for Wildlife
- **Telephoto Lens:** 100-500mm for distant subjects
- **High ISO Performance:** Clean images in challenging light
- **Fast Autofocus:** Tracking moving subjects precisely
- **Weather Sealing:** Protection in harsh environments

This collection celebrates the incredible diversity of wildlife while promoting conservation awareness through the power of photography.
