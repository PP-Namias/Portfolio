## Technical Approach

### Lighting Setups
- **Natural Light:** Large windows for soft, even illumination
- **Studio Strobes:** Controlled power and direction
- **Reflectors:** Fill light and shadow management
- **Background Selection:** Complementary colors and textures

### Camera Settings
- **Aperture:** f/1.2-f/2.8 for shallow depth of field
- **Shutter Speed:** 1/125s minimum for sharp portraits
- **ISO:** Kept low for maximum image quality
- **Focus:** Single point AF on the eyes

## Post-Processing Style

The post-processing maintains natural skin tones while enhancing the mood and atmosphere of each portrait. Techniques include:

1. **Color Grading:** Subtle warm/cool balance
2. **Skin Retouching:** Natural enhancement without over-processing
3. **Eye Enhancement:** Subtle brightening and contrast
4. **Background Separation:** Depth and dimension
