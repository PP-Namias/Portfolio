# Mountain Vista at Sunset

This stunning landscape photograph captures the ethereal beauty of mountain peaks bathed in golden sunset light. The dramatic cloud formations create natural leading lines that draw the eye through the composition, while the warm light contrasts beautifully with the cool mountain shadows.

## Technical Details

**Camera Setup:**
- **Camera**: Canon EOS R5
- **Lens**: RF 24-70mm f/2.8L IS USM
- **Focal Length**: 45mm
- **Aperture**: f/8
- **Shutter Speed**: 1/125 second
- **ISO**: 100
- **Focus**: Single point AF on foreground rocks

**Post-Processing:**
- RAW processing in Lightroom
- Graduated filters for sky exposure
- Luminosity masking for detail enhancement
- Color grading for warm/cool balance

## Story Behind the Shot

This photograph was taken during a three-day backpacking trip in the Rocky Mountain National Park. After hiking for six hours to reach this vantage point, I waited for nearly two hours for the perfect lighting conditions. The patience paid off when the clouds aligned perfectly with the mountain peaks, creating this dramatic composition.

The challenging part was balancing the exposure between the bright sky and the darker foreground. I used a graduated neutral density filter to help control the dynamic range, and later fine-tuned the exposure in post-processing using luminosity masks.
