## Technical Excellence

### Macro Techniques
- **Focus Stacking:** Multiple shots for extended depth of field
- **Ring Flash:** Even illumination for small subjects
- **Tripod Essential:** Stability for precise focusing
- **Live View Magnification:** Critical focus confirmation

### Environmental Considerations
- **Golden Hour:** Soft natural lighting
- **Wind Protection:** Shields for delicate subjects
- **Background Selection:** Clean, uncluttered compositions
- **Subject Positioning:** Optimal angles for detail revelation

## Equipment Details

The Canon RF 100mm f/2.8L Macro lens provides:
- 1:1 magnification ratio for life-size reproduction
- Image stabilization for handheld shooting
- Weather sealing for outdoor conditions
- Superior optical quality for maximum detail
