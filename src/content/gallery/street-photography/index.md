## Street Photography Approach

Street photography is about being in the right place at the right time, with the right mindset to capture authentic human moments in urban environments.

## Technical Approach

### Camera Settings
- **Fast Apertures:** f/1.4-f/2.8 for subject isolation
- **Higher ISOs:** 400-1600 for available light
- **Quick Shutter:** 1/125s+ to freeze motion
- **Single Point AF:** Precise focus on subjects

### Composition Techniques
- **Leading Lines:** Using architecture to guide the eye
- **Framing:** Natural frames within urban environment
- **Decisive Moment:** Capturing peak action or emotion
- **Environmental Context:** Including surroundings for story

## Post-Processing Style

Maintains authentic street photography aesthetics with subtle enhancements:
- Natural color grading
- Contrast adjustments for drama
- Minimal HDR processing
- Grain preservation for character
