## Photography Philosophy

### Cultural Sensitivity
- **Respectful Approach:** Always asking permission for portraits
- **Local Customs:** Understanding cultural norms and traditions
- **Authentic Moments:** Capturing genuine daily life experiences
- **Environmental Awareness:** Leaving no trace while exploring

### Technical Approach
- **Versatile Lens:** 24-105mm covers most travel scenarios
- **Weather Adaptability:** Sealed gear for challenging conditions
- **Light Weight:** Portable setup for extended journeys
- **Backup Systems:** Multiple memory cards and batteries

## Travel Destinations Featured

### Asia Pacific
- **Nepal:** Himalayan villages and mountain culture
- **Japan:** Traditional crafts and modern contrasts
- **Thailand:** Markets, temples, and coastal beauty
- **Indonesia:** Island life and volcanic landscapes

### Europe
- **Italy:** Renaissance architecture and countryside
- **Norway:** Fjords and northern lights
- **Greece:** Ancient ruins and island life
- **Iceland:** Geothermal wonders and dramatic coastlines

### Americas
- **Peru:** Andean culture and archaeological sites
- **Canada:** Wilderness and urban landscapes
- **Mexico:** Colonial towns and desert beauty
- **Chile:** Patagonian adventures and wine regions

Each destination offers unique photographic opportunities and cultural insights that enrich both the journey and the resulting images.
