## 🎨 Image Creation Guidelines

### Featured Image Templates

**Technical Tutorial Images**:
- Code editor screenshot with highlighted syntax
- Workflow diagram with your branding
- Before/after comparison screenshots
- Tool logos arranged professionally

**Project Case Study Images**:
- Architecture diagram with clean design
- UI mockups or final product screenshots
- Technology stack visualization
- Results/metrics infographic

**Career Insights Images**:
- Professional quote overlay on branded background
- Simple infographic with key points
- Professional headshot with article title
- Timeline or process visualization

### Image Specifications
- **Dimensions**: 1200x630px (optimal for social sharing)
- **Format**: PNG or JPG
- **File Size**: Under 500KB
- **Style**: Consistent branding and color scheme
- **Text**: Large, readable fonts (minimum 24px)

## ✅ Pre-Publication Checklist

### Content Review
- ✅ **Title**: Compelling and SEO-optimized
- ✅ **Excerpt**: Clear value proposition under 160 chars
- ✅ **Structure**: Logical flow with clear headers
- ✅ **Code**: All examples tested and working
- ✅ **Links**: Internal and external links verified
- ✅ **Images**: Optimized with alt text
- ✅ **Proofread**: Grammar and spelling checked

### SEO Optimization
- ✅ **Primary Keyword**: Naturally integrated
- ✅ **Headers**: Proper H1/H2/H3 hierarchy
- ✅ **Meta Description**: Keyword-rich excerpt
- ✅ **Internal Links**: Connected to related content
- ✅ **External Links**: Authoritative sources
- ✅ **Image Alt Text**: Descriptive and keyword-relevant

### Technical Standards
- ✅ **Mobile Responsive**: Readable on all devices
- ✅ **Loading Speed**: Images optimized for performance
- ✅ **Accessibility**: Proper heading structure and alt text
- ✅ **Code Formatting**: Syntax highlighting and proper indentation
- ✅ **Cross-browser**: Compatible with major browsers

These templates provide a solid foundation for creating consistent, professional content. Customize them based on your specific expertise and audience needs while maintaining the quality standards that establish you as a trusted authority in your field.
