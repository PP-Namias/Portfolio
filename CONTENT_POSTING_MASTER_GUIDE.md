## 🚨 Quality Control

### Before Publishing Checklist:
- ✅ **Spelling & Grammar**: Use Grammarly or similar
- ✅ **Technical Accuracy**: Verify code examples
- ✅ **Image Quality**: High resolution, compressed
- ✅ **Mobile Preview**: Test on phone
- ✅ **Links Working**: Test all external links
- ✅ **SEO Complete**: All meta fields filled

### Content Standards:
- **Minimum Length**: 500 words for tutorials, 300 for updates
- **Reading Level**: Accessible to intermediate developers
- **Code Examples**: Properly formatted and tested
- **Visuals**: At least one image per post
- **Structure**: Use headings, bullets, and sections
