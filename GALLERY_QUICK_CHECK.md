## 🎮 How to Use

### Mouse
- Click **LEFT button** (dark with blue ←) = Previous
- Click **RIGHT button** (dark with blue →) = Next

### Keyboard
- Press **←** = Previous
- Press **→** = Next
- Press **Esc** = Close

### Search
- Type: "Google", "AWS", "DAP", "DICT"
- Finds by organization
