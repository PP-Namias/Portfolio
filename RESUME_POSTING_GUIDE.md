# 📄 Resume Document Posting Guide

## 🎯 Purpose

Manage your professional resume document in Sanity CMS for easy updates and portfolio integration. This ensures your latest resume is always accessible to potential employers and collaborators.

---

## 📋 Step-by-Step Instructions

### Step 1: Prepare Your Resume

#### **Document Requirements**

**Format**: PDF (strongly recommended)

- Professional appearance
- ATS-friendly formatting
- High-quality export (300 DPI minimum)
- File size under 2MB

**Alternative Formats** (if needed):

- DOCX for editable versions
- TXT for keyword optimization

#### **Content Standards**

**Essential Sections**:

- Professional summary
- Technical skills
- Work experience
- Education
- Projects (with links to portfolio)
- Certifications

**Naming Convention**:

```text
PP_Namias_Resume_2024.pdf
```

### Step 2: Access Sanity Studio

1. **Go to**: <https://ppnamias.sanity.studio/>
2. **Sign in** with your Sanity account
3. **Navigate to**: "Resume" in the left sidebar
4. **Click**: "Create New Resume" or the "+" button

### Step 3: Upload Resume Document

#### **File Upload Process**

1. **Click**: "Upload" in the Resume File field
2. **Select**: Your prepared PDF resume
3. **Wait**: For upload completion
4. **Verify**: File displays correctly

#### **Document Metadata**

**Title**: `PP Namias - Full Stack Developer Resume`

**Description**:

```text
Current resume for PP Namias, Full Stack Developer specializing in AI automation, React development, and team leadership. Updated [current month/year] with latest experience at Wilshire Financial Network and recent project accomplishments.
```

**Version**: `2024.1` (increment as you update)

### Step 4: Resume Content Fields

#### **Professional Summary** (Required)

**Template**:

```text
Innovative Full Stack Developer with [X] years of experience specializing in AI automation, modern web applications, and team leadership. Proven track record of designing and implementing intelligent solutions that streamline business operations and enhance user experiences.

Currently serving as AI Developer and Team Leader at Wilshire Financial Network, where I design no-code AI chatbots and automation tools that have improved operational efficiency by [X]%. Expert in React, Node.js, TypeScript, and AI integration platforms.

Key achievements include leading cross-functional teams, developing CRM systems that serve [X] users, and creating AI agents that automate complex business workflows. Passionate about bridging the gap between cutting-edge technology and practical business solutions.
```

- Update years of experience
- Include specific metrics and achievements
- Mention current role and company
- Highlight your unique value proposition

#### **Work Experience** (Detailed)

**For Each Position**:

**Format**:

```text
**[Job Title]** | [Company Name]
[Start Date] - [End Date/Present] | [Location]

[Brief company description and your role context]

Key Responsibilities:
• [Responsibility 1 with specific technologies/outcomes]
• [Responsibility 2 with measurable impact]
• [Responsibility 3 with team/project scope]

Notable Achievements:
• [Achievement 1 with metrics/results]
• [Achievement 2 with business impact]
• [Achievement 3 with technical innovation]

```

**Example**:

```text
**AI Developer & Team Leader** | Wilshire Financial Network
March 2024 - Present | Remote

Leading AI automation initiatives for a financial services company focused on modernizing business operations through intelligent technology solutions.

Key Responsibilities:
• Design and deploy no-code AI chatbots using OpenAI API and n8n automation platforms
• Lead cross-functional teams in developing marketing materials and CRM integrations
• Architect AI agents that automate complex customer service and data processing workflows

Notable Achievements:
• Improved operational efficiency by 40% through automated customer service chatbots
• Led development of CRM system serving 500+ active users with real-time data synchronization
• Created AI-powered lead qualification system that increased conversion rates by 25%

Technologies Used: OpenAI API, n8n, React, Node.js, PostgreSQL, Docker
```

## ✅ Quality Checklist

### Content Quality

- ✅ **Clear Formatting**: Easy to scan and read
- ✅ **Quantified Results**: Specific metrics and achievements
- ✅ **Relevant Keywords**: Industry-standard terms
- ✅ **Current Information**: Up-to-date contact and experience
- ✅ **Error-Free**: No typos or grammatical mistakes

### Technical Standards

- ✅ **PDF Format**: Professional and consistent appearance
- ✅ **ATS-Friendly**: Simple formatting, standard fonts
- ✅ **Proper Length**: 1-2 pages maximum
- ✅ **Contact Information**: Professional email and LinkedIn
- ✅ **Portfolio Links**: Working links to projects and portfolio
